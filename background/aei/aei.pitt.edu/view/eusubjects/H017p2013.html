UNSPECIFIED (1950) This page or section contains documents on countries which may join the EU in the future. Current candidate countries: Albania, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Serbia, Turkey. Current potential candidate countries: Bosnia and Herzegovina, Kosovo. All EU documents present on the Archive of European Integration concerning these countries are listed here, beginning in the 1950s.
